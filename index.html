About

Scène turns a daily check-in into a cinematic memory. Your entries stay on your device. When you choose to generate a scene, the text and any photos you add are sent to Google's Gemini models to create your image and monthly reflections. Scène has no accounts, no ads, and no third-party analytics or tracking.
