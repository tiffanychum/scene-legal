How it feels

Check in, in seconds

Pick a colour on the mood wheel, name the feeling, set your energy. That's it — no forms, no friction, just a moment of honesty about your day.

Your mood, made cinematic

Scène turns your check-in into a scene: a generated image, a matching quote, and a song — a little piece of cinema that captures exactly how today felt.

A memory album, automatically

Every scene is saved to your calendar. Scroll back through the month and watch your moods become a diary you'll actually want to revisit.

See your month take shape

Your Arc reflects on the month behind you — your dominant moods, your top song, and the emotional shape of your days, written back to you.

Resonance — coming soon

See who else felt it too. A quiet feed of anonymous moods from around the world, so you know you're never really alone in how you feel.
