Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Scène (“Scène”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information. Scène is operated by CHUM Man Lee, an individual developer based in Hong Kong.

By downloading or using Scène (the “App”), you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Summary

  • Scène is a personal mood diary. Your diary entries and generated images are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not require an account, and we do not use advertising, third-party analytics, or tracking SDKs.
  • When you choose to generate a scene or a monthly reflection, the text you enter and any photos you add are sent to Google (via Firebase AI Logic / the Gemini models) to produce that content.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

2. Information we process

a. Information you provide

  • Mood check-ins — the feelings, words, moods, and any notes you enter.
  • Photos — optional images you add through the “Put yourself in the scene” feature (for example, a selfie or a meaningful object) so they can be woven into your generated image.
  • Generated content — the images and monthly reflections created for you.

b. Information processed automatically

  • Local app data — preferences and settings (such as your chosen theme and notification settings) stored on your device.
  • Local notifications — scheduled on your device; we do not operate a push-notification server that identifies you.

Scène does not collect your name, contacts, precise location, advertising identifiers, or browsing history, and does not include third-party analytics or tracking.

3. How we use information

  • To provide the core features of the App — generating your scenes, reflections, quotes, and song matches.
  • To store your diary on your device so you can revisit it.
  • To send you local reminders and “your scene is ready” notifications, if you enable them.
  • To respond to your support requests.

4. Third-party processing (Google / Firebase)

To generate images and written reflections, Scène uses Google’s Firebase AI Logic and Gemini models. When you request generation, the relevant text and any photos you add are transmitted to Google for processing and returned to your device. This processing is subject to Google’s terms and privacy practices:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy and Security
  • Gemini API Additional Terms of Service

Because generation relies on a third-party AI provider, please do not upload photos of other people without their consent, and avoid submitting sensitive personal information you would not want processed by a third party.

5. Storage and retention

  • Your diary entries, photos you add, and generated images are stored locally on your device until you delete them or uninstall the App.
  • You can delete individual entries in the App, or remove all of your data using the “Delete all data / reset” option in Settings.
  • Content transmitted to Google for generation is handled and retained by Google according to their policies linked above; we do not maintain our own copy on a server.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, or to object to certain processing (for example, under the EU/UK GDPR or the California CCPA/CPRA). Because your diary data lives on your device, you can exercise access and deletion directly in the App at any time. For any other request, contact us at sprout.app.support@gmail.com. We do not “sell” or “share” personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

7. Children

Scène is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can address it.

8. Security

We rely on your device’s security features and on Google’s security for data in transit and processing. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Availability by region

Scène is not offered in, and is not intended for use in, mainland China, Russia, or Türkiye. Availability may change and may be further restricted by the app store where you obtained the App.

10. International transfers

Google may process data in countries other than your own. Where required, such transfers rely on the safeguards described in Google’s policies.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, by notice in the App. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact
